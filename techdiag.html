📚 Guide diagnostic Bluetooth
🖨 Imprimante BT
• Imprimante thermique Bluetooth
• Service UUID: 0x1101 (SPP)
• Portée: ~10m
• Diagnostic: tester impression page test
⚡ Module HC-05
• Baud AT mode: 38400
• Baud data mode: 9600
• Commandes: AT, AT+NAME, AT+UART
• LED clignote = AT mode actif
📱 ESP32 Bluetooth
• BT Classic + BLE simultané
• UUID service personnalisé
• Portée: ~30-50m
• Vitesse: jusqu'à 3 Mbps
🎵 Audio Bluetooth
• Profils: A2DP, HFP, AVRCP
• Codec: SBC, aptX, AAC, LDAC
• BT 5.0+: portée ×4
• Latence: 40-200ms selon codec
